About Me

Project Manager - Data Analyst - Dog Mom

Hi, I'm Meaghan! Though new to the data analytics field, I was motivated to obtain my Masters in Business Intelligence and Analytics at Merrimack College by my passion for data driven decision making. While I am just starting out in the Business and Data Analytics field, I have over ten years' experience in the areas of event planning and project scheduling. As a project manager, I have striven to bring analytical insights to my department through the use of Excel and PowerBI. Whether it is analyzing options for post-Covid back to office hybrid models, or streamlining the policies and procedures for facilities operations and overhead, analytics has become a clutch tool for bringing insights and stability to an ever-changing world.

Example Projects

Below are some examples of recent project work.

Airline Delay Dashboard

I created a Power BI dashboard to assist the Department of Transportation in determining the cause of airline delays. In addition to visualizing the data, this project incorporated the use of DAX code. This project analyzed airline delay data over the course of two years to determine whether recently instituted policies had an impact on airline delays and to determine any recommendations to minimize future delays.

Data Science Salary Recommendations

Utilizing R Studio, I analyzed statistics for over 600 data scientist salaries to determine a competitive wage that will draw top talent for client's company. This project included API integration, as well as aggregating and visualizing the data using ggplot.
